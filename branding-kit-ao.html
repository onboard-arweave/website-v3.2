Minimum Size
To ensure the visibility and legibility of the logo,
the minimum size of the combination of glyph
and text is 85px and the glyph is 15px.
Layout
The AO logo system emphasizes
minimalsm and makes heavy use of
negative space. Adequate separation
and spacing should be used to achieve
balance between both logotype &
glyph, and scaling kept to a minimum
for overall effectiveness.
